Hayahay Beach Resort

About us

Hayahay Beach Resort is a tropical paradise situated in Aundanao, near the beautiful Canibad Cove Beach in Samal District. Here's what you need to know about this tranquil getaway:

    Establishment: Hayahay Beach Resort was established in 2009 and has been welcoming guests for over a decade. Its name, “Hayahay,” reflects the laid-back and serene atmosphere that awaits visitors. Perfect Getaway: If you're looking for an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, this resort is the perfect destination. Nestled amidst nature, it offers a blend of relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty.

    Amenities and Comfort:

    The resort provides an array of amenities to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable. Whether you're lounging on the beach, exploring the surroundings, or simply unwinding, Hayahay Beach Resort has something for everyone.

    [Hayahay Beach Resort is committed to creating a harmonious balance between providing exceptional guest experiences and preserving the natural beauty of Samal Island. Our mission revolves around three core principles:

    Environmental Stewardship:

    We are passionate about protecting the environment. Our commitment extends beyond our resort boundaries to the entire island. We actively participate in beach clean-up initiatives, promote responsible tourism practices, and educate guests about marine conservation. By fostering a deep connection with nature, we aim to inspire others to become stewards of our precious coastal ecosystems.

    Community Empowerment:

    We believe in uplifting the local community. All our employees are residents of Pangangan Island, ensuring that we contribute directly to the livelihoods of our neighbors. Through job opportunities, skill development, and fair wages, we empower our staff to thrive and create a positive impact on their families and the community. Guest Experience with a Local Flavor: Hayahay (which means “relax” in the local dialect) is not just a name; it's our ethos. We want every guest to experience genuine relaxation and rejuvenation. Our resort offers a blend of comfort, seclusion, and authentic island vibes. From beautiful beaches to fun activities, we create memories that resonate with the soul.

    What Makes Us Unique:

  • Secluded Paradise:
    • Our location away from the city ensures a peaceful escape. You'll find solace amidst nature, away from the hustle and bustle.
  • Local Engagement:
    • By employing local residents, we foster a sense of community and authenticity.
  • Beautiful Beaches:
    • Our pristine shores invite you to unwind, explore, and connect with the natural world.
  • Fun Activities:
    • Whether it's snorkeling, kayaking, or simply lounging by the beach, we offer experiences that celebrate the island’s beauty.]
